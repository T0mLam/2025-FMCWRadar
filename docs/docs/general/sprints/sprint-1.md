# Sprint 1 — 19/01/26 - 25/01/26
This document describes our first Sprint of TB2 and reflecting on the project structure of TB1

## Reflecting on the negatives of TB1
- We met frequently, but conversations weren’t focused on sprint goals or deliverables.
- We didn’t have a clear, coherent vision for the project’s direction.
- Communication and coordination were weak; some teammates struggled to find appropriate tasks.
- We didn’t fully understand our meta-project goal (providing feedback to TI on the development process).

## How to succeed in TB2
- Use the correct channels — the **Client Liaison** handles communications with the client.
- Set clear deadlines, especially when others are blocked by your work.
- Appoint a **Project Manager** to structure meetings and hold us accountable.
- Hold weekly standups (Friday/Monday) to review progress and plan the next week.
