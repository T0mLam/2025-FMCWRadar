## Sprint Focus and Reflecting on Beta VIVA 
For this sprint we started by reflecting on our beta VIVA presentation; we received great feedback on our presentation, their were many positives, such as the slides, content, and how we showcased the technology. The two key areas we can improve are the speed at which we present (we talked a bit too fast!) and showcasing how the work we have done differs from what our client has given us. We will take this feedback into account in order to improve our presentation for the final VIVA.
