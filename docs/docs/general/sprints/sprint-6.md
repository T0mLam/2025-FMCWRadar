## Sprint Focus and Reflecting on the Beta Release to Client
For this week our main focus was to take the feedback given to us from our beta presentation to the client and to improve both our project and presentation in preparation for VIVA at the end if this sprint. The feedback our client gave us was really encouraging highlighting the positives of our approach and giving us tips on how to further improve the model accuracy. This has given us a better vision for the future of the project and what we can do to elevate it.
