# Ethics

**Ethics Route:** Route B - Lecturer Approval

**Date Submitted:** 22nd October 2025

---
This project follows **Route B** because data is anonymous at the point of collection and no personally identifying information is recorded. The system uses an FMCW mmWave radar to capture motion signals only; it does not record images or audio. During captures names, contact details, and other personal data are not collected. Participants are consenting adults from within the team. The privacy-preserving nature of mmWave sensing underpins our ethical position: because no images or audio are recorded and identifiers are excluded, the risk of re-identification is substantially reduced relative to camera-based systems.
