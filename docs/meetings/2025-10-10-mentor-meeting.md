## Workflow & Git Practices
* **Main Branch Rule:** Do not commit or merge directly into the **`main`** branch.
* **Feature Branching:** All development work must be done on a feature branch **branched off of `dev`**.
* **Branch Naming:** Ensure feature branches are given **descriptive and useful names** (e.g., `feature/add-docusaurus-research`).
* **Commit Consistency (Contribution Tracking):** This is about team equity. Make sure that all team members are **contributing a similar volume of work** (commits or lines of code) to the project.
    * Commits should not be too "uneven" across team members.
    * If contributions become significantly skewed toward one or two members, we will have to **justify** the workload distribution for the project review.
