## Kanban & Branch Workflow 

### Branch Naming Convention
Adopt this structure for all new branches:

`category/issue-number/short-description`

* **Example:** `docs/#55/update-readme`

### General Workflow 

1.  **Branch Cleanup:** It's a good practice to **delete branches after they are merged** to keep the repository tidy.
2.  **Kanban Discipline:** Ensure that both **Issues** and **Pull Requests (PRs)** clearly reflect their current **status on the Kanban board** (e.g., *To Do*, *In Progress*, *Review*).
3.  **Issue Detail:** Make sure **all issues have a clear description and a milestone** assigned. Even for docs and meting notes - just good practice
