# Date of Collection: 27/01/2025
### Room used: 1.15 Queens Building
### Data Collected
- Standing: 2mins x 4 people
- Sitting: 2mins x 4 people
### Setup Measurements:
- Distance to chair: 3m
- Height of board: 1.5m
- Angle of board: 0 degrees

### Summary:

The data collected for standing contained large amounts of noise that was recorded above the board. Perhaps due to being position directly under a whiteboard.
Lying data could not be collected due to the height and lack of angle in the board's positioning.
Sitting data contained many rows that had too few points indicating that the sensor was not sensitive enough.

### Improvements:

- Position the board away from any obstructions that could be recorded by the sensor
- Increase the distance between the board and the chair
- Angle the board downwards for a better view of the lower portions of the subject
- Increase the motion sensitivity of the board
